## How to play

Walk with A/left_arrow, D/right_arrow and jump with W/up_arrow.
Drag and drop objects from the upper part of the level
to the lower part in order to find a way to avoid the scary obstacles.

If you are feeling creative, feel free to post
a screenshot of the main level on social media
and show the world how you arranged the objects.

Take your time to enjoy the visuals
and music!
